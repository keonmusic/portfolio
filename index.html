About

Keon Wu

Keon Wu is an Australian composer specialising in film, games, television, and cinematic media. His work spans orchestral drama, sci-fi atmosphere, and emotional storytelling — shaped by an obsession with how music transforms our understanding of a scene.

Beyond composition, Keon handles the full production chain — writing, producing, mixing, and mastering — bringing a singular creative vision from first note to final delivery.
